Our Story

The story of Grandma Pat’s Cafe begins with one woman’s love for java and her desire to share that love with others. Patricia "Pat" Lebo—fondly known as Pat—opened the doors of our cafe in 1985, inspired by the warmth and comfort of her favorite coffee house in Trenton PA.

"Hospitality inspired by faith, one cup at a time."
